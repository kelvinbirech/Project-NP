Understanding Your Speed Test Results

Download Speed

How fast you can receive data from the internet. Important for streaming, browsing, and downloading files.

Upload Speed

How fast you can send data to the internet. Essential for video calls, uploading files, and cloud backups.

Ping/Latency

Response time of your connection. Lower is better for gaming, video calls, and real-time applications.
